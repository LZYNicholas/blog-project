Who Is Nicholas?

My Story

I started growing interest in Cybersecurity when I first saw how cool hacking was in the movies. Looking at how cool hacking was, I wanted to be in an IT course so that I could kickstart my journey. I looked into Diploma in Information Technology as the course cover many aspects of the IT and is able to build my foundation on IT before jumping into Cybersecurity.

During my studies in NYP, I scored extremely well for the cybersecurity modules and realised that I have a talent for Cybersecurity. On the final year of my studies, I chose Cybersecurity as my specialisation.
