Who Is Nicholas?

Hi, I am Nicholas. I graduated from Nanyang Polytechnic (NYP) with a Diploma in Information Technology. I have a strong interest for Cybersecurity since young and I enjoy keeping myself fit by going to the gym and playing basketball.

How I Started My Journey

When I first saw how cool hacking was in movies, I became interested in cybersecurity and wanted to enroll in an IT course so that I could begin my journey. I looked into the Diploma in Information Technology because it covers many aspects of IT and allows me to build a solid foundation in IT before diving into Cybersecurity.

During my studies at NYP, I excelled in the cybersecurity modules and discovered that I have a talent for cybersecurity. In my final year of studies, I chose Cybersecurity as my specialisation.

What Can I Find Here?

I create posts on Cybersecurity topics as well as other content that I may find interesting.

My Hopes On Creating This Blog

Creating this blog site allows me to archive information and knowledge that I have gained throughout my IT journey. I hope that by sharing this information and knowledge, my dear readers will benefit and learn a little bit more about IT and security.
